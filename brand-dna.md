Color Palette
Primary Blue (Brand & Actions): #1E75C4 (The vibrant blue seen in the logo, primary buttons, and highlighted headline text).

Secondary Dark (Typography & High Contrast): #1A202C (The deep, near-black charcoal used for the main headlines).

Accent Light Blue (Backgrounds & Badges): #E1EFFE (The soft, muted ice blue used for the pill badge and active states).

Neutral Gray (Body Copy & Muted Text): #6B7280 (The clean slate gray used for subheadings and navigation links).

2. Google Fonts
Headings: Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans (Perfect for bold, tech-focused SaaS platforms; it matches the geometric, modern sans-serif feel of their current headline).

Body: Roboto or Inter (Offers exceptional readability at smaller sizes for directory cards, bios, and descriptions).

3. Tone of Voice
Professional yet Community-Driven: The copy is welcoming and ecosystem-focused ("vibrant community," "grow and fund their dreams together"), but maintains a sharp, authoritative, and clean tech-startup boundary ("Bangladesh's #1 Founder Directory"). It balances networking utility with collaborative inspiration.

4. Catchy Hero Headlines
Option 1 (Action-Oriented): "Discover, Connect, and Build with Bangladesh's Brightest Founders."

Option 2 (Value-Driven): "The Ultimate Network for Dhaka’s Next Generation of SaaS Builders and Investors."

Option 3 (Community-Focused): "Your Gateway to Bangladesh's Most Elite Startup Ecosystem starts here."
